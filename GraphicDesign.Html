Color Theory Diagram

For one of my graphic design classes, we had to create a color theory diagram that explained the relationships between the different hues on the color wheel as well as explain tint, shade, saturation, temperature, and complementary colors. We also had to incorporate real images that we photographed. I chose to use famous skyscrapers because it made the diagram more interesting and cool to look at.
