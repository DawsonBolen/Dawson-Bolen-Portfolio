My Work

I interned for the Yiddish Arts and Academics Association of North America (YAAANA) as a web developer. They are a non-profit organization who's main goal is to preserve and teach Yiddish language and culture. I helped them maintain and update their Yiddishland California website with many various updates.


My tasks varied greatly but were mostly updates. I would often create pages for events, add events to the upcoming events page, and create the newsletter.


The top picture is on the events page that I would often add updates to. I added the Yiddishland at La Jolla Art and Wine Festival that links to the page that I created for that event.
